EGALITARIANISM

EQUALITY AMONGST ALL

We in Team ACM, Strongly believe in and Practice Egalitarianism. As for us Equality amongst all is a necessity without which as a Family, we cannot prosper and help others prosper. It is our Pride as well as our Identity. We provide equal oppurtunities to each one of our core members as well as the participants to grow and prosper in their field of interest and help them achieve their goals with proper supervision and guidance.

INTEGRITY

ON MORALS WE BUILD

In Team ACM, We promote and maintain strong moral principles as on the bedrock of the moral principles we grow and help others grow. We are honest to ourselves and to our team as well as those we serve. Honesty has always helped our society to rectify our mistakes and learn from them and we look forward to carry on the same in the coming future. As a team we respect as well as demand Integrity to keep our roots strong.

ZEALOUS

PASSION WITH PRIDE

We follow our aim , that is, serving those we can with the best of our capabilities, with full dedication, focused approach, and above all with Passion. Passion has been and will always be the reason behind the miracles. We persue each and every act with passion so that maximum number of people may benifit from us and we as a society may fulfil our aim.
